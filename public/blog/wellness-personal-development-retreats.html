Sanctuary of the Spirit

"Host your next Yoga or Personal Development retreat in a sanctuary of Art. Silence, nature, and historic beauty to support deep work."

The success of a wellness or personal development retreat depends intrinsically on the venue. A sterile hotel or a noisy convention center can break the flow state necessary for transformation.

Le Salon des Inconnus offers the antidote. Here, silence is respected, art inspires introspection, and the surrounding nature grounds the experience.

Practice Spaces

Our grand Victorian lounge transforms into a majestic yoga or meditation hall. The gardens and yurt offer open-air alternatives for grounding and connection.

Nourishment & Care

Access a chef's kitchen for your healthy caterers, or use our creative culinary services. On-site: massotherapy and spa to integrate bodywork.

Why Choose Le Salon?

  • ✦ Total Privacy: Option to privatize the estate for your group (up to 20 people).
  • ✦ Energy of Place: An 1898 manor charged with history and creativity, conducive to emotional breakthroughs.
  • ✦ Accessible Nature: Walking trails and river just steps away for moments of solitude and integration.
