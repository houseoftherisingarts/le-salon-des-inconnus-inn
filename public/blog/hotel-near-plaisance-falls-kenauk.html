The Perfect Nature Base Camp

Explore the wild beauty of Kenauk and the Falls, then retreat to sanctuary.

Petite-Nation is vast. To explore the majestic Plaisance Falls and the wild nature of Kenauk Nature, you need a central point.

**Le Salon des Inconnus** in Namur is that anchor. We are strategically located to allow you to radiate towards all the natural gems of the region.

The Perfect Itinerary

  • Day 1: Picnic and exploration at Plaisance Falls. Return to the Salon for a gourmet dinner and a fireside evening.
  • Day 2: Fishing or hiking at Kenauk Nature. Return to the inn for a spa session under the stars.
  • Comfort: Don't sacrifice your sleep. Our cozy beds guarantee you'll be fit for tomorrow's adventure.
