Stay & Play at Heritage Golf

"Walk from the course directly to your Victorian room."

Heritage Golf Club in Notre-Dame-de-la-Paix is an Outaouais hidden gem. But after 18 holes, the last thing you want is a long drive home.

Le Salon des Inconnus is your closest neighbor. Located in Namur, just minutes from the clubhouse, our historic inn is the natural extension of your relaxing day.

The "Stay & Play" Experience Reimagined

  • Absolute Proximity: You could almost hear the irons clinking from our terrace. Finish your round, and be in a bubble bath in under 5 minutes.
  • Private Clubhouse Vibe: Our Victorian lounge, with its vinyl bar and fireplace, offers the perfect atmosphere to debrief your game over a drink.
  • Sports Massage: Our on-site massotherapy services are ideal for relaxing shoulders and backs after a day of swinging.
  • Gastronomy: Don't settle for a snack bar. Enjoy our creative cuisine or catering events for a memorable dinner.
