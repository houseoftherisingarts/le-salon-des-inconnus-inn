The Montpellier Medieval Festival is a highlight of the year. For a weekend, reality is suspended. Knights duel, bards sing, and magic feels real.

But when the gates close for the night, where do you go? A beige hotel room with fluorescent lights? Don't break the spell.

Your Sanctuary in Namur

Just minutes from the festival grounds, Le Salon des Inconnus awaits. Our inn is steeped in history, a Victorian manor from 1898 that feels like it belongs in a storybook.

  • ⚔️ The Great Hall Vibe: Our main lounge, with its dark wood, crackling fireplace, and curiosities, is the perfect setting to recount your day's quests.
  • 🎲 Games & Lore: We have a collection of board games (yes, including Gwent-style games) and a piano for the musically inclined.
  • 🌙 The Mystic Yurt: For those seeking a nomadic experience under the stars, our luxury Yurt offers the perfect blend of rustic and comfort.

A Feast for the Soul
