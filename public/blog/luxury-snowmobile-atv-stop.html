The Luxury Pit-Stop in Namur

Secure parking, warm beds, and great atmosphere. The best stop on Route 323.

Outaouais snowmobile and ATV trails are legendary. But accommodation options are often basic.

**Le Salon des Inconnus** changes the game. Strategically located in Namur, we offer a high-end stopover for riders who don't want to sacrifice comfort.

Why Stop Here?

  • Secure Parking: Large parking space for your trailers and machines, directly on site.
  • Gear Drying: Dedicated space to dry boots, coats, and helmets overnight. Start fresh and dry.
  • Comfort: After a day in the cold or dust, enjoy a hot shower, our spa, and hotel-quality beds.
  • Club Vibe: Our lounge with vinyl bar is the perfect place to share the day's stories over a drink.
