The Hidden Gem of Outaouais

Browsing Tourisme Outaouais? Discover the region's most artistic secret in Namur.

Outaouais is full of treasures, but some remain well-kept secrets. **Le Salon des Inconnus** is one of them.

More than a hotel, it is a destination in itself. Located in Namur, in the heart of Petite-Nation, our Victorian manor offers a cultural and artistic alternative to large hotel complexes.

The "Salon" Experience

  • Unique Architecture: Sleep in a heritage home from 1898, carefully restored to preserve its soul while offering modern comfort.
  • Artist Center: The inn's walls are a living gallery. Discover works by local and international artists during your stay.
  • Strategic Position: We are the perfect central point to explore Montebello, Parc Omega, Lac Simon, and Tremblant without packing and unpacking every day.
  • Intimate Atmosphere: With a limited number of rooms, we guarantee tranquility and personalized service you won't find anywhere else.
