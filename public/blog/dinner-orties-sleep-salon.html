Dinner at Orties, Sleep at Le Salon

Extend the magic of your gourmet meal in Ripon. Stay in a Victorian manor nearby.

**Café des Orties** in Ripon is a must for foodies in the region. But after a great meal and a few glasses of natural wine, driving home isn't ideal.

Complete your gourmet evening with an equally exceptional overnight stay at **Le Salon des Inconnus** in Namur, located just nearby.

The Gastronomic & Artistic Duo

  • The Quiet Drive: A short scenic drive separates the restaurant from our inn. It's just enough to digest while admiring the stars.
  • Complementary Vibe: The bohemian and local spirit of Café des Orties blends perfectly with the artistic and historic ambiance of the Salon.
  • The Morning After: Wake up slowly with our barista coffee and enjoy our gardens before heading out.
  • Packages & Events: Keep an eye out for our occasional collaborations during Petite-Nation festivals and cultural events.
